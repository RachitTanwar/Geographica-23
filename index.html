Geographica' 23

The Department of Geography, Kamala Nehru College proudly hosts the departmental fest titled “GEOGRAPHICA”. The celebration, also known as “Geography Day”, is aimed at promoting, facilitating, and disseminating geographical knowledge and awareness. Specific geographical themes have been taken up each year including ‘Eco-Urbanisation’, ‘Agriculture’, ‘Carbon-350’, ‘Gender Studies’, ‘Delhi 350’ and many more. Geography Day is made successful by commencing with an educational lecture on the theme selected theme that year following various inter-college competitions such as quizzes, debates, photography and videography, talent shows, different forms of dance, singing and other activities. Panch Tatva

Our theme revolves around the 5 elements of nature.

Vayu

The Air element is gentle, uplifting, nurturing, healing, freeing and balancing.

Jal

This element governs fluidity, purification, nourishment, and controls the movement of energy, fluids and the physical body.

Agni

The Fire element is hot, bright, active, dynamic, strengthening, and stimulating. This element governs passion, anger, ambition, desire, willpower, courage, confidence, self-expression, creative thinking, and leadership. .

Aakash

It represents pure consciousness, intuition, creativity, imagination, inspiration, faith, love, compassion, empathy, peace, joy, bliss, truth, purity, wisdom, and transcendence.

Prithvi

This aspect of nature governs groundedness, stability, strength, permanence, patience, fertility, and security.
